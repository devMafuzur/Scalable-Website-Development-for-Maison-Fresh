Our Story & Ethos

Maison Fresh brings together the finest organic produce and artisanal foods in Toronto's most historic market setting. We believe in the craft of food curation, sourcing directly from local farms and trusted producers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Our vision blends old-world market charm with modern French elegance, creating an experience that honors both tradition and innovation. Every product tells a story of craftsmanship, from heritage vegetables to artisanal prepared foods.
